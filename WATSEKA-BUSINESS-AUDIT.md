### Key Takeaways

**~65% of Watseka businesses have NO web presence at all.** No website, no social media, nothing findable online except maybe a Yellow Pages listing. This is the core opportunity for visitwatseka.com.

**Downtown businesses are the most underserved.** Many Walnut St shops, salons, restaurants, and service providers have zero online presence. These are the businesses visitors would most want to find.

**Tier 1 businesses are your natural partners.** They already invest in their online presence and would likely welcome a free backlink from visitwatseka.com. Start with the Chamber of Commerce members.

**The "Places to Go" page could be huge.** A simple local business directory on visitwatseka.com — organized by category with name, address, phone, and links where available — would be the only comprehensive online directory for Watseka businesses.
