Welcome to Watseka, Illinois

Small Town Roots,
Big Community Heart.

Watseka is the county seat of Iroquois County, Illinois — a small town with deep roots and a big heart. Explore the charm of Downtown Watseka, from our Saturday morning Farmers Market to local shops, restaurants, and community events.
