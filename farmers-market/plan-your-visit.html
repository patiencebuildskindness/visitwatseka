First Time at the Market?

Here's what a perfect Saturday morning in Downtown Watseka looks like.

1
🌅
Arrive Early
The best vendors and freshest produce go fast. Aim to arrive at or shortly after 7 AM for the best selection.
2
🚶
Walk the Whole Market
Take a full lap first before you buy — you'll want to see everything before you decide. There's always something unexpected.
3
🛍
Shop & Explore
Pick up fresh produce, grab a pastry or a hot breakfast, browse handmade goods, and chat with the makers behind the tables.
4
☀️
Stay & Enjoy
Live music, face painting, family activities, and good company. There's no rush — make a morning of it.
🅿️

Parking

Free parking is available throughout Downtown Watseka. Street parking is plentiful along Walnut St and surrounding blocks. Additional free parking is available at the First Trust Parking Lot on the corner of 3rd St & Walnut St, and the DMV Parking Lot directly behind Silo Pub & Eatery.

Please note that vehicles are not permitted in the market parking lot between 7:45 AM and 12:05 PM on market days. Plan to park in the public lot or on the street before the market opens.

🚻

Restrooms

Public restrooms are available on site for vendors and visitors throughout market hours, located inside the Silo Pub & Eatery | Homestead Venue — a welcome indoor amenity on warm summer mornings.

🛒

What to Bring

  • 👜 A reusable bag or basket for your haul
  • 💵 Cash is always accepted — check with your vendor ahead of time to confirm whether they take cards
  • 🕶 Sunscreen & sunglasses — it's an outdoor market
  • 🧢 On super warm days, an umbrella, sun hat, or cap goes a long way to beat the heat
  • 👟 Comfortable shoes for strolling
  • 🐾 Your leashed dog — well-behaved dogs are welcome!
🐾

Pet Policy

Dogs are welcome at the market! We love four-legged visitors. Please make sure your pup is leashed and well-behaved around other people and animals.

As a courtesy to vendors and fellow visitors, please clean up after your pet and keep them from vendor tables and food areas.

♿

Accessibility

The Downtown Watseka Farmers Market is held in an outdoor parking lot with flat, paved surfaces — accessible for wheelchairs and strollers throughout the market area. Handicap parking is available nearby. If you have specific accessibility needs or questions, please reach out to us in advance at dtwatsekafarmersmarket@gmail.com and we'll do our best to assist.
