About the Market

The Downtown Watseka Farmers Market is a beloved Saturday morning tradition in the heart of Iroquois County. Every week from June through October, vendors gather at 121 W Walnut St to offer fresh produce, handmade goods, artisan foods, specialty items, and more — all sourced locally and crafted with care.

We are a family-friendly, free community event that brings neighbors together, supports local growers and makers, and keeps dollars circulating right here in Watseka. Organized as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, every dollar supports the market and the community it serves.
