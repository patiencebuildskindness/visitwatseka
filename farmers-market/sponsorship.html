About the Market

The Downtown Watseka Farmers Market is a beloved Saturday morning tradition in the heart of Iroquois County. Every week from June through October, vendors gather at 121 W Walnut St to offer fresh produce, handmade goods, artisan foods, specialty items, and more.

We are a family-friendly, free community event that brings neighbors together, supports local growers and makers, and keeps dollars circulating right here in Watseka.

Why it matters: With a population of roughly 5,000, Watseka doesn't have the foot traffic of a city market — but it has something more valuable. The people who shop here are your neighbors, your customers, and your advocates. Sponsoring the market puts your business in front of every one of them, every Saturday, all season long.

About the Sponsorship

Sponsoring the Downtown Watseka Farmers Market is one of the most direct ways a local business can invest in the community it serves. Your sponsorship helps fund market operations, vendor support, and the ongoing effort to make Downtown Watseka a destination worth visiting every week.

Our commitment to sponsors: Every sponsorship benefit we offer is one we can deliver. We keep our promises small enough to keep and meaningful enough to matter. No fine print, no surprises.
