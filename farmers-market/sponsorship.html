About the Market

The Downtown Watseka Farmers Market is a beloved Saturday morning tradition in the heart of Iroquois County. Every week from June through October, vendors gather at 121 W Walnut St to offer fresh produce, handmade goods, artisan foods, specialty items, and more — all sourced locally and crafted with care.

We are a family-friendly, free community event that brings neighbors together, supports local growers and makers, and keeps dollars circulating right here in Watseka. In a town where community connection matters more than ever, the Farmers Market is one of the few places everyone shows up.

Why it matters: With a population of roughly 5,000, Watseka doesn't have the foot traffic of a city market — but it has something more valuable. The people who shop here are your neighbors, your customers, and your advocates. Sponsoring the market puts your business in front of every one of them, every Saturday, all season long.

About the Sponsorship

Sponsoring the Downtown Watseka Farmers Market is one of the most direct ways a local business can invest in the community it serves. Your sponsorship helps fund market operations, vendor support, and the ongoing effort to make Downtown Watseka a destination worth visiting every week.

In return, your business receives real, tangible visibility — not an ad that gets scrolled past, but genuine recognition from a community that values businesses who show up for them.

Our commitment to sponsors: Every sponsorship benefit we offer is one we can deliver. We keep our promises small enough to keep and meaningful enough to matter. No fine print, no surprises.

All social media recognition is distributed across our platforms — including Facebook, Instagram, and visitwatseka.com — so your brand reaches the broadest possible local audience without any single channel feeling oversaturated.
